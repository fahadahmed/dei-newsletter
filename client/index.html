A Note from Alex

As we wrap up the year, let's take a moment to acknowledge the progress we've made in promoting diversity, equity, and inclusion (DE&I) at EY Oceania. The world is changing fast and we want to make sure EY is one of the leaders of that change. Last month we ran a successful Neurodiversity November campaign, and now we are in the midst of our annual 16 Days of Activism Against Gender-based Violence campaing. Let's keep up the momentum in creating a safe and inclusive workplace.

Alex Wilson
EY Oceania
Diversity, Equity and Inclusiveness Leader

Neurodiversity November 2023

Last week we concluded the Neurodiversity November 2023. We are proud to have launched EY Oceania’s first Neurodiversity Strategy. Our month-long campaign included six events and had provided opportunities for XXXX EY staff to learn about neurodiversity, the strengths and skills neurodivergent people and how to be a better ally to those who are neurodivergent.

New Workplace Adjustments process

In November 2023 we launched our new person-centred approach to Workplace Adjustments (WPA). Whether you require adjustments relating to accessibility, health or other aspects of your role, this new process will provide you with a more straightforward approach to your request, along with access to a Case Manager to support you through the process. Refer to the new Workplace Adjustments SharePoint to find comprehensive information about the process, guidelines for making requests, and who to contact for assistance.
