A Note from Alex

The world is changing fast and we want to make sure EY is one of the leaders of that change. At EY we believe only the highest performing teams, teams who maximise the power of different opinions, perspectives and cultural references will succeed in the global marketplace. Leveraging, Diversity, Equity & Inclusiveness (DE&I) is one of the ways we will gain that advantage.

Alex Wilson
Associate Director
Leading the Oceania DE&I team
